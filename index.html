About Us

At Community Mapping & Biodiversity, we are a dedicated group of environmental enthusiasts
from County Dublin and County Mayo, united by our passion for preserving the natural beauty
of Ireland's night skies. Our mission is to raise awareness about the growing issue of
light pollution and its impact on our environment and communities.

Light pollution not only dims the stars above but also disrupts ecosystems,
affecting nocturnal wildlife and altering natural behaviors. In Ireland, with its rich heritage
and breathtaking landscapes, preserving the integrity of our night skies is crucial
for both ecological balance and cultural heritage.

We believe that by mapping light pollution levels across Ireland—starting with our own
communities in Dublin and Mayo—we can inspire change and promote practices that reduce
unnecessary artificial lighting. Through education, community engagement, and innovative
mapping technologies, we aim to highlight the importance of darkness in our environment
and advocate for sustainable solutions.

Join us in our journey to bring back the stars and protect the nocturnal wonders
of our beautiful country, Yours Truly Ayush, Brian and Joseph.
